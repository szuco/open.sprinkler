# Summary

I needed a case for my TIAO Smart Sprikler controller that uses OpenSprinkler PI, but I wasn't able to find anything that really fit the bill. 

This design uses magnets to hold the top portion of the case to the bottom after the controller board is installed. The magnets are .125 in magnets and I was able to get 100 of them off of ebay for pretty cheap. 

I printed this with my Ultibots K250. 

http://www.diygadget.com/tiao-smart-sprinkler-pi-16-station-sprinkler-controller-open-source-desktop-mobile-app.html
